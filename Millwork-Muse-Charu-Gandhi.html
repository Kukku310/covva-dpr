🪵 MILLWORK SPOTLIGHT

  • Hyper-Storage: Millwork integrated into "wasted" spaces—over bathtubs and within custom daybeds.
  • Silk-Wrapped Joinery: Coat cupboards wrapped in Philip Jeffries Pewter Silk & Metallic Silk. A shift away from exposed timber.
  • Hidden Lacquer Surprise: High-gloss colored lacquer used on the *interior* of wardrobes for a pop of surprise.

🪨 MATERIALITY

  • Wallcoverings on MDF: Using Dedar Silk Moiré and metallic silk as a decorative laminate alternative.
  • Sculptural Metal: Custom brass handles by Barbara Fermat acting as the primary decorative accent on minimalist doors.

💡 R&D SIGNAL

Trend: The "Wrapped Millwork" aesthetic. Charu Gandhi is moving away from timber grains toward metallic and fabric-textured cabinetry.

COVVA Action: We can replicate this using our **Cold Press** to mount high-end wallcoverings (Silk/Moiré) onto MDF panels, then protecting them with a **matte PU spray** in the paint booth. This allows us to offer "Elicyon-style" textures without sourcing rare veneers.
